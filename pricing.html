Buyer Costs & Fees Guide

Transparent pricing for bidders and buyers on the NovaBid platform.

Purpose

This guide outlines the costs and fees buyers may encounter when using NovaBid. Our commitment is to maintain clear, upfront pricing with no hidden charges.

Core Principle: Zero Buyer Fees

NovaBid operates on a simple model: buyers bid and win without paying platform fees.

There are no:

  • Bidding fees
  • Registration fees
  • Winning bid premiums
  • Membership charges
  • Hidden transaction fees

When you bid $100, you pay $100 if you win—nothing more.

What Buyers Pay

1. Winning Bid Amount Only

If you win an auction, you pay the exact amount of your winning bid to the vendor. NovaBid does not add fees on top of this amount.

2. Vendor-Defined Costs (If Any)

Vendors may include costs such as:

  • Shipping or delivery fees
  • Taxes or regulatory charges
  • Customization or handling fees

These costs are disclosed before bidding begins and are set by the vendor, not NovaBid. They are reflected in the lot details and bid structure.

Payment Methods

Payment is handled directly between the buyer and vendor following the auction close. NovaBid facilitates secure handoffs but does not process payments directly unless escrow is configured by the vendor.

Supported payment arrangements include:

  • Bank transfer
  • Credit/debit card (if supported by vendor)
  • Cryptocurrency (if supported by vendor)
  • Escrow services (vendor-configured)

Any fees associated with these payment methods are determined by the vendor or payment processor, not by NovaBid.

Buyer Protections

NovaBid's transparency extends to buyer safeguards:

  • All costs are disclosed upfront in lot details
  • Immutable bid and event records provide auditability
  • Disputes are resolved based on verifiable auction data
  • Escrow options available for high-value transactions

We do not surprise buyers with retroactive fees or hidden charges.

Why This Matters

Traditional auction platforms often add buyer premiums, success fees, or membership costs on top of the winning bid. NovaBid eliminates this complexity.

What you bid is what you pay. This approach builds trust, reduces friction, and keeps the focus on competitive pricing—not fee navigation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does NovaBid charge buyers anything?

No. Buyers pay only their winning bid amount and any vendor-disclosed costs (such as shipping).

Are there account or membership fees?

No. Creating an account and participating in auctions is free.

Can vendors add fees after the auction?

No. All costs must be disclosed before bidding begins. Post-auction fee additions are prohibited.

What if I need a refund or dispute resolution?

NovaBid provides dispute resolution based on verifiable auction data. Refund policies are set by vendors and disclosed in advance.
